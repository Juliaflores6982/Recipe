Easy 3 Ingredients Mug Cake Recipe

You can make a warm, delicious mug cake in just minutes using only three ingredients: Nutella, an egg and flour.

Ingredients

  • Nutella 3 tablespoons (provides flavor and moisture)
  • Egg 1 large (binds the ingredients together)
  • Flour 3 tablespoons (all-purpose, oat, or almond flour works)

Instructions

  1. Mix Ingredients: In a microwave-safe mug, combine Nutella, egg, and flour. Stir until smooth and fully incorporated.
  2. Microwave: Cook on high for about 60–90 seconds, depending on your microwave’s power. The cake should be set on top but slightly gooey in the center.
  3. Serve: Let it cool for a minute, then enjoy directly from the mug. Optionally, top with extra Nutella, peanut butter, or a scoop of ice cream for added indulgence.

Tips for the Best Mug Cake

Texture

Using Nutella gives the cake a moist, slightly gooey texture. Substitutions: You can replace Nutella with other sweet spreads like Biscoff cookie butter or peanut butter, and use different flours for gluten-free options

Quick Prep

The entire process takes about 5 minutes, making it perfect for a fast dessert or snack.

Customization

This 3-ingredient mug cake is ideal for satisfying sudden dessert cravings without complicated steps or long ingredient lists, and highly customizable to your taste preferences
